REACO MEETING SESSION

This is our weekly online session where we learn new things in the industry to stay updated. It brings experienced resource personnel to share in-depth insights, information and industry and professional knowledge.

An intensive workshop session for professionals in the built industry including architects, engineers, contractors, surveyors, planners, estate developers etc. We have the BPM for the Professionals and the BPM for Artisans.
