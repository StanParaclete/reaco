Board Member Profile

Mr Abdul-Latif Seidu Bogobiri - Board Member

Mr Abdul-Latif Seidu Bogobiri is an Applied Chemist and Occupational Health, Safety and Environmental Specialist with 24 years of industry experience having led and been part of institutions of great repute where he has contributed immensely towards the growth of those institutions, the industries they belong to and the nation as well as making impact in the global industrial space.

He holds three Masters Degrees in the areas of Marketing; Occupational Health, Safety and Environment; and in Theology and Ministry.

He formerly worked with the Obuasi Mine of Anglogold Ashanti, one of the top three gold mining companies in Ghana which is a global gold mining company, formed in 2004 by the merger of AngloGold and the Ashanti Goldfields Corporation.

Mr Bogobiri is the former General Manager of Kaleo Royale Limited, a company which was in distribution of Surfline data connectivity devices and scratch cards. He doubled as the Health and Safety Advisor to the board of the same organization.

He once served as a key staff of Healthy Life Beverages; and also the Sales and Marketing Manager of the Business and Financial Times (B&FT), whose tenure saw great improvements and sustained brand awareness and increase in revenue of the newspaper. Latif led the team to organize a successful Ghana Energy Summit (GES) in 2022. He was instrumental in the B&FT’s organization of the “Ghana Economic Forum” , “CEO Breakfast series” and many other events and outreach programmes by the B&FT . He went on to successfully offer his highly sort-after services as National Sales and Marketing Manager of Truworth Pharmaceutical Ltd.

Mr Abdul-Latif Seidu Bogobiri is currently the Executive Director of Bogolat Business Consult which is a limited liability company business entity which offers services including pharmaceutical supplies, eatery joints, men & women clothing outlines & groceries.

Aside his busy schedules in the business circles, he brings to bear his expertise as a Television panalist on subjects of national interest where he is an authority and also issues bothering on religion, socio-economic development and health and general well being.

He is a Member of the Programme for Christian -Muslim Relations in Africa of the Christian Council of Ghana (PROCMURA) and the Ghana Baptist Convention Representative on Committee. Again, he is the Africa Programme Director of Ambassador of Hope Global Ministries lnc with its head office in the United State of America, SC.

He is the Vice Board Chair of Crisis Intervention Africa Foundation, a non governmental organization with an aim of supporting families and corporate organizations in trauma management and intervention

Bogobiri is Baptist with Rehoboth Baptist Church, Kweima-Accra A life member with Beautiful Gate Chapter.

Bogobiri, Seidu Abdul-Latif is married to Judith Puhiyin Bogobiri and they are blessed with three children namely Wekem, Wekia and Wedam.
